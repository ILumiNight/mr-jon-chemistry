ILLUMINATING LEARNING

Hello students! Welcome to my new project!

I thought back to how I used to study for my examinations while I was in university, and realised that videos often helped me to recap and revise key concepts I had learnt. Hence, I am embarking on a project to capture some important concepts online, in a space easily accessible by you!

Through this resource, I hope that chemistry becomes something less daunting to you. I firmly believe that every one of you have the potential to excel, and I will do my best to support that growth!

Caveat: This is not sufficient as a standalone for revision. I do not cover 100% of the content required; this is just a supplementary tool!

To get started, navigate to one of the chapters using the navigation bar above. Happy learning!

-- Mr Jon Ong
